About us

Stencila Team

Stencila team comprises of staff from different backgrounds. That allows us to bring different points of view to our work which results in outputs useful for diverse group of users. We share the support for open source technologies and lowering the barrier for learning how to code.

Nokome Bentley,
Founder and CEO

Nokome is the creator and the main driving force behind Stencila. Before he launched the company, he was a fisheries scientist focusing on the development, evaluation and selection of fisheries management procedures. During his research work Nokome experienced many struggles with software development, problems with lack of interoperability of the existing tools and explored many ways of addressing them. One of the solutions that he came up were stencils which gradually evolved towards Stencila suite. Nokome is based in Aotearoa New Zealand.

Ben Shaw,
Senior Software Engineer

Ben is a Software Engineer with many years' experience across a range of fields and industries.
